MyTaskKing

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MyTaskKing collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our web, Android, iOS, Windows, and macOS applications.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how your workspace is configured, MyTaskKing may collect the following information:

  • Account details such as name, user ID, email address, role, designation, and profile information.
  • Work activity such as tasks, reports, attendance check-ins, lunch break times, logout reports, and calendar events.
  • Communication data such as chat messages, channel messages, mentions, call history, meeting room details, and shared files.
  • Uploaded content such as images, documents, voice notes, recordings, and other files you choose to share.
  • Device and technical information such as device type, platform, app version, IP address, browser, crash/debug information, and authentication/session data.
  • Notification identifiers such as Firebase Cloud Messaging device tokens used to send push notifications.
  • Microphone/audio data only when you choose to use calling, voice notes, speech-to-text, or audio features.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide and improve MyTaskKing services, including to:

  • Create and manage user accounts, employee roles, clients, teams, and workspaces.
  • Deliver task management, attendance, reports, calendar, chat, file sharing, and meeting features.
  • Send work-related notifications, mentions, task updates, meeting alerts, and call alerts.
  • Authenticate users, protect accounts, prevent unauthorized access, and maintain audit/activity logs.
  • Store and display files or images uploaded by users in chats, tasks, meetings, or other workspace areas.
  • Improve app reliability, diagnose bugs, and maintain service performance.

3. App Permissions

MyTaskKing may request permissions only when needed for a feature:

  • Microphone permission is used for voice calls, voice notes, speech-to-text, and audio features.
  • Camera and photo/file access may be used when you upload profile images, chat images, documents, or task attachments.
  • Notification permission is used to send push notifications for tasks, chats, calls, attendance, and meeting updates.
  • Network access is required to connect the app to MyTaskKing backend services.

You can disable permissions in your device settings, but some app features may stop working.

4. Third-Party Services

MyTaskKing may use trusted third-party providers to operate app features:

  • Firebase is used for push notifications and related app messaging services.
  • Agora is used for real-time voice, call, and meeting features.
  • Cloudflare R2 and/or Cloudinary may be used to store uploaded files and images.
  • Google Gemini or other AI providers may be used when AI assistance features are enabled by the workspace.

These providers process data only as needed to deliver the requested service features.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • With users inside your authorized workspace as required for collaboration features.
  • With service providers who help operate hosting, storage, notifications, calling, analytics, or security.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the service.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Workspace data is retained for as long as needed to provide the service, meet business requirements, maintain security, or comply with legal obligations. Workspace administrators may delete users, chats, tasks, calls, files, and related data according to their organization’s policies.

If you want your account or data deleted, contact your workspace administrator or email us using the contact information below.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information, including authenticated API access, protected user sessions, role-based access controls, and secure storage practices. No internet service can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to protect your data responsibly.

8. Children’s Privacy

MyTaskKing is intended for business and productivity use. It is not directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will revise the “Last updated” date on this page. Continued use of MyTaskKing after changes means the updated policy applies.
